### 1. $f(x, y) = x^2e^{2y}$

$$
\frac{\delta}{\delta x} f(x,y) = 2xe^{2y}
$$
$$
\frac{\delta}{\delta y} (f,x) = 2x^2e^{2y}
$$

### 2. $f(x, y) = \ln(x+y^2)$
$$
\frac{\delta}{\delta x} f(x, y) = \frac{1}{x + y^2}
$$
$$
\frac{\delta}{\delta y} f(x, y) = \frac{2y}{x + y^2}
$$

### 3. $f(x, y, z) = e^{xyz} + z$
$$
\frac{\delta}{\delta x} f(x, y, z) = yze^{xyz}
$$
$$
\frac{\delta}{\delta y} f(x, y, z) = xze^{xyz}
$$
$$
\frac{\delta}{\delta z} f(x, y, z) = xye^{xyz} + 1
$$
